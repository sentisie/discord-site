General Questions

What is Resonix?

Resonix is a Discord music bot that allows you to play music from SoundCloud in your Discord server's voice channels.

Is Resonix free to use?

Yes, Resonix is completely free to use with all its features available to everyone.

How do I add Resonix to my server?

Click the "Invite" button in the navigation menu and follow the Discord authorization process to add Resonix to your server.

Music & Playback

Which platforms does Resonix support?

Currently, Resonix supports SoundCloud. Support for other platforms will be added in future updates.

How do I play music?

Use the /play command followed by a SoundCloud URL or song name. Make sure you're in a voice channel first!

Can I control the volume?

Yes, use the /volume command followed by a number between 0 and 100 to adjust the volume.

Troubleshooting

The bot isn't responding to commands

Make sure the bot has proper permissions and is online. Try using the /ping command to check if the bot is responsive.

Music won't play

Ensure you're in a voice channel and the bot has permissions to join and speak in that channel.
